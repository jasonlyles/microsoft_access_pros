About


Brad has over 30 years experience in the software industry, originally developing real-time simulation software for the defense industry. He worked over 10 years for United Parcel Service, managing large software projects and developing Microsoft Access Database and internet applications. He later developed web sites for both non-profit and for-profit businesses, including consulting on the business aspects of increasing sales and creating a positive user experience.

Brad earned his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Virginia Tech in 1978, and his MBA from Loyola College of Maryland in 1990.

Brad has developed in-office applications to track members, track donations, handle sales, and manage contact lists. His web sites specialize in interactive applications to both attract visitors and to generate and process sales. What makes his services unique is the ability to integrate the online data with the office database to allow order fulfillment, invoicing, sales reports, and ongoing customer contact.

Brad has served on several non-profit boards, including Smoke Free Maryland, The US Hydrofoil Association, and USA Water Ski. Brad is a partner in for-profit online businesses, so he has a clear understanding of your business needs.

Software Expertise: Microsoft Access, SQL Server, Asp.NET, C#, Visual Basic, Office365, Microsoft Office, MySQL, HTML/CSS, Javascript, C/C++, Microsoft Visual Studio
