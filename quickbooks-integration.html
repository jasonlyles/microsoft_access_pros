Microsoft Access Integration with QuickBooks

Save time, Increase Accuracy


Send or Receive Customer Information, Invoices, Payments, Credit Card Transactions, Purchase Orders, Sales Receipts, Vendors, Bills, and other QuickBooks Items or transactions seamlessly between your desktop application and Intuit QuickBooks. Your choice: synchronize with each change or on demand at the end of the day, week, or month.

Works with QuickBooks Pro, QuickBooks Premiere, and QuickBooks Enterprise, 2002 or newer. We use the QuickBooks SDK for the fastest integration. You could learn it yourself, but why would you want to? It's complicated, and we've already figured it all out.

We can integrate QuickBooks into your existing Microsoft Access desktop application or online application or create a new one. We customize every integration to make sure you get exactly what you need.

If you have your own Microsoft Access developer, or you are a Microsoft Access developer proficient in VBA, see the QuickBooks Link library for developers. This Microsoft Access developer library allows the same integration with QuickBooks.
