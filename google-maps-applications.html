Google Maps Application from Microsoft Access or Sql Server Database

Case Study: The City of Port St. Lucie, FL needed to maintain millions of dollars worth of trees and shrubs. Their maintenance department needed to know which trees and shrubs needed pruning or replacing, how to manage their watering systems, and where trees or shrubs are located throughout the city. Working with Masuen Consulting and using the Google Maps application shown in the images below, they are able to save hundreds of thousands of dollars in watering costs and plant replacement costs, while increasing the attractiveness of their city. This application allows them to search for trees and shrubs, edit them from their mobile devices in the field, and import new planting areas with data gathered from a Trimble GPS.

Microsoft Access Pros can integrate Google Maps into your existing Microsoft Access desktop application or web-based application or create a new one. We customize every integration to make sure you get exactly what you need, including custom icons, and specific features.
